HARD CONSTRAINTS
1. Do not change actual manuscript content.

2. Preserve all details.

3. Never edit files inside any `examples` subfolder unless explicitly instructed.

4. You may remove references to example files from `paper/main.tex`.
5. Do not leave template placeholder content mixed into final manuscript chapters.
6. If `merge/` is empty or missing, stop immediately and report that as a blocker.

Stage 4: Cleanliness pass on `paper/` codebase

Stage 5: Final validation and equivalence checks
- Validate content parity between `merge/` and final `paper/`.
